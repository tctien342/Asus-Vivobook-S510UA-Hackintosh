Not happy with your VivoBook's sound quality?

There is two things you can do about that:

1. Software: eqMac - a system-wide audio equalizer

For more info, please navigate to this release's folder "post macOS Installations/[Optional]/better sound with system-wide Equalizer eqMac" for all information

2. Hardware: a small custom piece of sound-reflecting metal underneath

To fix the bad sound with the VivoBook on one's lap because the loudspeakers are pretty much 100% covered (ASUS WTF ..), I used 0,8 mm sheet metal from the hardware store, had it punch-cut, milled and rounded in a metal shop, drilled holes for the screws, then glued the piece to the case bottom with strong double-sided carpet tape (sticky on both sides). Much better sound (both, quality and volume) now:

Hope this helps someone.
